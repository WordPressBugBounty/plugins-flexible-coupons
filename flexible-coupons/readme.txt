Flexible PDF Coupons - Gift Cards & Vouchers for WooCommerce - plugin to design and sell PDF gift cards, vouchers, or coupons in your store.

== Description ==

**Flexible PDF Coupons - Gift Cards & Vouchers for WooCommerce - plugin you can use to design, sell and send by e-mail gift cards, vouchers or coupons in PDF format.**

WooCommerce gives you the possibility to use discount coupons in your store. Now, you may let your customers buy such gift cards - with WooCommerce coupon on it, right in your store. Such gift cards and vouchers may be also sent as a gift.

### PDF Gift Cards, Coupons, Tickets, Vouchers for WooCommerce

* PDF gift cards are using WooCommerce discount coupon system,

== Installation ==

To install the plugin and get it working:

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Use the PDF Coupons -> Templates  screen to manage coupon templates

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How can I sell gift cards and vouchers in WooCommerce? =

The plugin lets you change any product to a gift card, voucher, or ticket with WooCommerce coupon codes ⭐!

1️⃣ First, **download the plugin**.

2️⃣ **Log into your WordPress backend** and go to the Plugins menu.

3️⃣ Then, **install the plugin** by uploading it or using a search bar (Flexible Coupons) ⚙️.

4️⃣ Go to the **PDF Coupons menu to set a template** for your WooCommerce PDF gift card, voucher, or ticket 🪟.

5️⃣ Finally, set a new or existing **WooCommerce product to become a gift card, voucher, or ticket**. And that's it 😊.

From now on, you will sell WooCommerce gift cards, vouchers, or tickets. Of course, you may use PDF coupon templates for selling more WooCommerce products as gift cards and vouchers 🚀!

Of course 😊.

You may use the plugin to:

* Design your own PDF coupons and sell them as **gift cards, tickets, or vouchers in WooCommerce** 🛍️
* **Increase your revenue** with new gift card products in WooCommerce 💰
* Let your WooCommere customers **buy vouchers, gift cards, or tickets as gifts** 🎁
* **Test new WooCommerce products and marketing ideas** and with gift cards 💝
* **Sell PDF vouchers with variable products** in WooCommerce 💲
* **Track the coupon conversion** and gift card sales in WooCommerce 📈
* Automatically print the WooCommerce **order data on the PDF gift cards and vouchers using shortcodes** 🪟
* **Send PDF vouchers by mail** and adjust the gift card email templates in WooCommerce 📨
* **Add multiple coupon templates** for better gift cards and WooCommerce product customization ⭐
* **Let customers personalize gift cards** directly on the WooCommerce product page 😊
* **Grow your WooCommerce with PDF vouchers, tickets, and gift cards** on special days 🎄

= Are there any requirements for Flexible PDF Coupons - Gift Cards & Vouchers for WooCommerce ⚙️? =

First, you need to **turn on coupons** in the WooCommerce &rarr; Settings &rarr; General.

You should also have **allow_url_fopen ON and GD Support enabled** in your server environment.

Finally, the gift card template editor requires a minimal browser version e.g.:

* Microsoft Edge - 83.0.478.45
* Chrome - 83.0.4103.97
* Internet Explorer 11.0.9600.18860
* Opera 68.0.3618.156
* Firefox 77.0
* Safari 13.1.1

= How to create PDF gift cards automatically for WooCommerce orders? =

You may set **automatic PDF gift card creation for WooCommerce order statuses**: processing, pending payment, on hold, or completed 🚀.

**You can also choose not to generate the PDF vouchers automatically**, but to make them manually on the WooCommerce order screen.

= What is a coupon template? =

The plugin **uses a coupon template to create PDF gift cards, vouchers, or tickets in WooCommerce** according to your design 🎨.

You can enter some text, images, styling, and shortcodes.

The gift card template uses shortcodes to **insert specific WooCommerce or plugin-related data dynamically** ⭐.

= Can I use multiple coupon templates for my WooCommerce PDF vouchers? =

Of course 😊.

You may choose the specific PDF voucher template on the WooCommerce product edit screen. It will be visible after enabling the PDF coupon checkbox in the Product data section. The coupon template will be used to **create PDF vouchers for simple and variable WooCommerce products** 🚀.

What's more, in the PRO version of the plugin, you can set **different coupon templates per each product variation** ⭐!

= How to make a WooCommerce product a gift card, voucher, or ticket? =

The plugin lets you change any product to a gift card, voucher, or ticket with WooCommerce coupon codes ⭐!

1️⃣ First, **install and activate the plugin** ⚙️.

2️⃣ **Log into** your WordPress backend 🔐.

3️⃣ Then, go to the PDF Coupons menu to **set a template for your WooCommerce PDF gift card, voucher, or ticket** 🪟.

4️⃣ Next, **edit a new or existing WooCommerce product** (simple and variable) 😊.

5️⃣ Go to the Product Data section and **enable the PDF Coupon checkbox** ✅.

6️⃣ **Decide about the coupon settings** and choose the appropriate PDF coupon template 📚.

7️⃣ Finally, save the changes and **see new types of WooCommerce products** (gift cards, vouchers, and tickets) 😊.

Yes 😊. The plugin will work for simple and variable products.

What's more, in the PRO version of the plugin, you can set **different coupon templates per each product variation** ⭐!

= Why does the plugin use the WooCommerce Coupon codes? =

Because the WooCommerce coupon codes are by default the built-in feature of your store. That means that you may use the same place for managing your coupons in WooCommerce and coupons for gift cards. Also, you will be able to use similar settings, edit the WooCommerce coupon if you need to, or export the data about all WooCommerce coupons.
